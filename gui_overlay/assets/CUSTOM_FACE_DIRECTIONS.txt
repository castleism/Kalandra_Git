=================================================================
           KALANDRA OVERLAY: CUSTOM FACE & GRAPHICS MANUAL       
=================================================================

This application supports custom visual overrides for both the outer
Mirror Frame and the central floating AI Orb core.

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Drop your custom PNG images directly into this folder.
2. Rename your files exactly to match the following names:
   - Outer Mirror Frame:   mirror_of_kalandra.png
   - Floating AI Orb/Face: divine_orb.png

3. Restart the overlay application to load your textures.

Note: If no files are detected, the system automatically draws high-
fidelity vector fallbacks. Transparent backgrounds (.png) are highly
recommended to maintain the immersive transparent overlay aesthetic.
