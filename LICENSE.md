## Why this is NOT GPL

Earlier we considered bundling Path of Building (GPL-3.0), which would have forced
this whole project to be GPL. We changed course: **Path of Building is installed
by the user, and Kalandra drives that separate copy at arm's length** (a
subprocess over stdio). Calling a separate, user-provided GPL program is "mere
aggregation" and does not place this app under the GPL. Nothing from PoB is
redistributed here. (This is an explanation, not legal advice.)
